WitcherCraft

A faithful recreation of The Witcher universe within Minecraft. Signs, Alchemy, Monsters and more.

What is WitcherCraft?

WitcherCraft is a NeoForge Minecraft mod that brings the world of The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt into the Minecraft engine. Cast Signs, brew potions, apply oils, hunt monsters from the Witcher bestiary, and progress through a deep RPG skill tree.
