Getting Started

Tips and tricks for new WitcherCraft players.

Here are some tips on how to play WitcherCraft. If you are just starting with the mod this page may be helpful.
First Steps
  • If you have any problems you can type /wiki in chat. A message with a link to this wiki will pop out.
  • After spawning into the world the first thing you want to do is find or build a shelter and gather materials — just as in standard survival. Don't go monster hunting during your first few nights.
  • Proper equipment is necessary. An iron armor set and iron weapons are the minimum for taking on monsters. Even then, some can be challenging in melee. A bow or crossbow is your best friend early on.
  • WitcherCraft monsters are very deadly and quick. If one spots you there is little chance of escape. Only engage fights you are confident you can win.
  • Use Signs. At first it may be easy to forget they are in the game, but Signs are powerful in many situations — especially Quen for survivability.
Installation

WitcherCraft requires NeoForge to run. To install:

  • Download and install NeoForge from the official NeoForge website.
  • Place the WitcherCraft .jar file into your Minecraft /mods/ folder.
  • Launch Minecraft using the NeoForge profile.
Key Bindings

WitcherCraft adds two custom keybinds:

  • Tab — Open the Sign Menu to select your active Sign.
  • R — Cast your currently selected Sign.
  • B — Open the Alchemy Menu to brew potions, oils, and bombs.
  • Esc / Pause Menu — Access Character stats, Skill Tree, Bestiary, Glossary and Meditation.
