Witcher Gear

Six school armor sets, each promoting a distinct combat playstyle.

This page is exclusively about Witcher School Gear. Every set consists of 4 pieces: Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings and Boots.

In WitcherCraft there are gear sets from 6 Witcher schools, harder to craft than standard Minecraft armors. Every Witcher Gear set grants a School Effect that promotes a certain playstyle. Mixing sets is possible but the School Effect only activates when a full set is worn.

Armors can be repaired with Leather Scraps.
